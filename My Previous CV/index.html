BACKGROUND

I was born in Hatay/Turkey in 1985. After completing Primary and High School Education, I started the Turkish Air Force Academy in 2001 and graduated in 2005 with the rank of Lieutenant.
Cadet 4th Class before graduation from TurAFA

I was appointed to the 2nd Main Jet Base Çiğli/İzmir for flight training and graduated in 2007 as a pilot. I trained here with SF-260D, T-37B/C and T-38A/B aircraft.
SF-260D Training at 2nd Main Jet Base Çiğli/İzmir

Between 2007 and 2008, I completed the Transition Training on the F-5 aircraft at the 3rd Main Jet Base Konya. I graduated from here with the first place.
Receiving the First place award from Brigadier General A. Bertan Nogaylaroğlu, 3rd Main Jet Base Commander

Subsequently, I completed the F-16 Training between 2008 and 2009 at the 4th Main Jet Base, 143rd Squadron Ankara.
My photo with my Flight Instructor while F-16 Training

Between 2009 and 2012, I carried out the duties of Wingman and 2 Ship Leader at the 5th Main Jet Base, 152nd Squadron Merzifon.
With my friends in 152nd Squadron

During this squadron, I deployed at the Sigonella Air Base on the island of Sicily in Italy (Operation Unified Protector) to fulfill the duty of the weapon embargo imposed by NATO against Libya and served for about 40 days. I won a medal (NATO Non-Article 5 medal for Operation Unified Protector) for this mission.
Sigonella Deployment

I passed the Staff Officer exam in 2012 and graduated from the Turkish Air War College in 2014 with the rank of Air Staff Officer.
Turkish Air War College graduation with my Family

Between 2014 and 2016, I carried out the duties of 4 Ship Leader and Instructor Pilot at the 9th Main Jet Base 191st Squadron Balıkesir.

I deployed at Ghedi Air Base / Italy in 2014 and Buchel Air Base / Germany in 2015 for the Cross-Servicing Exercise.
I'm the pilot on this F-16 aircraft.

I have participated in Anatolian Eagle Exercise many times in my Air Force life.
Anatolian Eagle Exercise 2010
